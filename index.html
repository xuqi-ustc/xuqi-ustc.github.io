Biography

I'm currently a in the School of Mircoelectronics, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I received my PH.D. in Electronic Science and Technology from University of Science and Technology of China in 2018.

My research interests include AI for EDA, Reliability Design for 3D-IC, Fault tolerance for Neuromorphic Computing System.[My Resume] [Google Scholar]
